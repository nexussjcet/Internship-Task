About the Event

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and creators from around the world to innovate and build amazing solutions.

Whether you're a beginner or a pro, this event is your chance to showcase your skills, learn new things, and connect with tech enthusiasts globally.
