About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour online hackathon bringing together developers, designers, and creators to innovate and build impactful solutions in a high-energy collaborative environment.

Join us for a weekend of creativity, learning, and global connection — whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hacker!
