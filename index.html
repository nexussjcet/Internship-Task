About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour hackathon bringing together developers, designers, and creators to build, innovate, and push boundaries. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out, there's a space for everyone here.

Join us for a weekend of inspiration, collaboration, and creative breakthroughs. Expect workshops, networking, mentorships, and exciting prizes!

Schedule

June 20: Opening Ceremony, Team Formation, Hackathon Begins
June 21: Mentorship, Workshops, Development Continues
June 22: Final Submission, Judging, Closing Ceremony
