About HackSphere 2025

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour online hackathon uniting developers, designers, and creators from every corner of the world. Whether you’re a seasoned coder or a first-time hacker, this is your chance to innovate, collaborate, and push the boundaries of technology.

Join us for an unforgettable weekend filled with creativity, learning, and networking. Compete for exciting prizes, attend expert-led workshops, and connect with a vibrant community passionate about building the future.

Note: Registration fee is ₹500 per head. Basic amenities, food, and stay will be provided to all participants.

General Rules

  • Each team can have a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 5 students.
  • The hackathon will run for 48 hours, starting from June 20, 2025, 6:00 PM IST to June 22, 2025, 6:00 PM IST.
  • All projects must be developed during the hackathon period. Pre-existing code is not allowed.
  • Teams must submit their project and a short demo video before the deadline.
  • All communication and submissions will be handled online.
  • Plagiarism or use of unlicensed materials will lead to disqualification.
  • Respectful and inclusive behavior is expected from all participants.
  • Organizers’ decisions regarding judging and prizes are final.
