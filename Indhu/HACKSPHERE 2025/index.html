About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and creators from all around the world. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a hobbyist — this is your chance to turn ideas into innovation.

Build something meaningful, solve real-world problems, and connect with a global community of tech enthusiasts — all from the comfort of your home. Join us online and let’s build the future, together.
