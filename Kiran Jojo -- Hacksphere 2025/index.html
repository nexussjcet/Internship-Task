About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour virtual hackathon uniting developers, designers, and innovators to push the boundaries of creativity and technology.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or a first-timer, HackSphere is the place to collaborate, build, and make an impact.
