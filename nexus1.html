About HackSphere

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour online hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and creators from around the world. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned innovator, this event is your platform to push boundaries, build impactful projects, and win exciting prizes.

Over the span of two days, you'll team up, brainstorm, code, and present your solutions to real-world challenges. Connect, collaborate, and compete in an electrifying atmosphere of innovation and creativity.
